Worker's Detector Operation

Follow these steps to operate the worker's detector:

  1. Step 1: Turn on the detector device.
  2. Step 2: Position the detector in a suitable location.
  3. Step 3: Wait for the detector to initialize.
  4. Step 4: Once initialized, the detector will start capturing worker's attendance automatically.
  5. Step 5: To view attendance records or make adjustments, access the management interface on the accompanying software.
  6. Step 6: When finished, turn off the detector device.
